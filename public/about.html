About

BINI is a Filipino pop phenomenon with 8 talented members: Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena.

The girls are dominating Filipino music charts with hits like “Salamin, Salamin” and “Pantropiko,” and are gaining international attention.
